Explore Puerto Princesa

A stunning paradise with natural beauty, rich culture, and unforgettable experiences.

Top Tourist Spots

Underground River

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this natural wonder offers a surreal boat ride through its cave system.

Honda Bay

Known for its crystal-clear waters and stunning islands, Honda Bay is the perfect spot for island hopping and snorkeling.

El Nido

A beautiful beach town famous for its limestone cliffs, hidden lagoons, and vibrant coral reefs.

Puerto Princesa Airport

The gateway to Palawan, offering access to the stunning natural wonders and eco-tourism destinations of the island.

Sabang Beach

A serene beach famous for its beautiful sands and clear waters, perfect for swimming, relaxing, and picnicking.

Twin Lagoons

A unique natural phenomenon with two lagoons, one fresh and one saltwater, separated by a rock formation that you can swim through.

Nagtabon Beach

A hidden gem perfect for surfing, relaxing, and enjoying breathtaking sunsets.

Iwahig Firefly Watching

An enchanting evening activity where visitors can witness the beauty of fireflies lighting up the mangroves.

Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park Trails

Beyond the famous river, there are nature trails within the park where visitors can see unique flora and fauna.

Baker's Hill

A popular destination in Puerto Princesa known for its delicious baked goods, gardens, and scenic views. Perfect for families and tourists looking to relax and enjoy local treats.

Plaza Cuartel

A historic park filled with stories of bravery from World War II. Plaza Cuartel offers a glimpse into the rich history of the city.

Palawan Wildlife Rescue and Conservation Center (Crocodile Farm)

A wildlife conservation center that houses and showcases crocodiles and other animals. This site provides education on conservation and wildlife protection in Palawan.

Top Food and Restaurants in Puerto Princesa

KaLui Restaurant

Famous for its fresh seafood and artistic ambiance, KaLui is a must-visit for an authentic Filipino dining experience.

Badjao Seafront Restaurant

Enjoy seafood dishes with a breathtaking view of the mangroves and the sea, offering a peaceful and scenic dining experience.

Kinabuch's Grill and Bar

A lively spot for dining and entertainment, Kinabuch’s is popular for its local specialties and relaxed atmosphere.

Rene Saigon

Specializing in Vietnamese cuisine, Rene Saigon offers a fresh and delicious alternative for those seeking bold and flavorful dishes.

Ka Inato

Ka Inato serves delicious local dishes with a twist, offering a truly Filipino experience for food lovers. Try their crispy binagoongan and other traditional favorites.

The Baker's Hill

A famous bakery and restaurant, The Baker's Hill offers a variety of freshly baked goods, as well as hearty Filipino meals, and is a popular stop for tourists looking to try local pastries and snacks.
